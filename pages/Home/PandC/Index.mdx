# People & Culture Subgroup Overview

Welcome to the People & Culture section of the FEDS Operational Manual. This subgroup serves as the strategic core of FRC Team 201, managing the initiatives that ensure the team's long-term viability, community presence, and internal health.

The People & Culture subgroup is responsible for bridging the gap between our technical objectives and our mission to inspire STEM engagement. We develop and execute the strategies that support our team's growth, professional image, and impact within the *FIRST* community and beyond.

<br />
<strong>Core Responsibilities</strong> 

<p>This subgroup's responsibilities are multifaceted, focusing on both internal team development and external relations. We cultivate leadership, manage communications, secure financial resources, and direct the team's outreach and advocacy efforts. Our work ensures that the team operates efficiently and sustainably.</p>

We do not simply manage administrative tasks; we build the foundational structure that enables team-wide success and amplifies our impact year after year.

<br />
