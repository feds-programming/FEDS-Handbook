# Welcome to People & Culture!

Welcome to the People & Culture section of the FEDS Handbook! Here, you will find all the resources and tools you need to understand the culture, community, and STEM initiatives of FRC Team 201. The People & Culture subgroup encompasses the FEDS FRC 201's Safety, Business, Advocacy, Outreach, Fundraising, Social-Media, and Impact initiatives, all of which are further described in upcoming sections.

The **Tools & Resources** section covers the important resources we use including our **Sponsor Scouting Tool / Sponsor Acquisition Resources**, **Email drafts to contact companies and other FIRST teams**, and **Impact Award Resources**. The **FEDS' Projects** section covers our events, marketing, and team-building aspects of our team, in addition to going over our team's Impact Award portfolio and our efforts towards advancing our team's mission in 4 categories: **Operation FIRST**, **Operation Outreach**, **Operation Global**, and **OPEX** (Operation: Performance & Excellence).
