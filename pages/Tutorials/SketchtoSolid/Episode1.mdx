## ğŸï¸ Chapter 2: Things That Go 'Round & 'Round

### Motors: The Heart of the Robot
We use **Brushless** motors because they are efficient and powerful.

*   **Krakens (X60, X44)**: The heavy hitters.
*   **Minions**: Small but mighty.
*   **NEOs**: The reliable standard.

### Shafts & Bearings
**Why 1/2" Hex?**
Because round shafts slip. Hex shafts lock. **Hexagons are the bestagons.**

**Bearings:**
They hold the shaft in place but allow it to spin. Without them, you're just drilling a hole in metal and hoping for the best (spoiler: it melts).
