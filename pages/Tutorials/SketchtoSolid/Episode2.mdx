## üß† Chapter 1: The Philosophy of Winning

### Quality over Quantity
It is better to do **one thing perfectly** than to do everything poorly. A robot that only climbs but does it 100% of the time is better than a robot that shoots, climbs, and spins plates but fails 50% of the time.

### Game Analysis: A History Lesson

The chassis is not just wheels. It houses the **Central Electrical Hub** (Battery, PDH, RoboRio) and localization sensors (Limelights). If the chassis fails, the robot is a brick.
