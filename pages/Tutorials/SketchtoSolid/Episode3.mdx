### Materials: The Soft Touch
*   **Rigid Intakes** (Polycarb/Aluminum) are for **Compliant Game Pieces** (Foam balls, inflated cubes).
*   **Compliant Intakes** (Rubber wheels, flex tubing) are for **Rigid Game Pieces** (Hard plastic cones).
*   *Opposites attract!*

### CAD Highlight: The 4-Bar Linkage
**The Problem:** How do I deploy an intake outside the frame but pack it inside for the start of the match?
**The Solution:** The 4-Bar Linkage.
*   Use geometric constraints in sketches to simulate the motion.
*   Ensure it stays within the "Frame Perimeter" when retracted.
